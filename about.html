About MYnCryptoCoin (MYN)

MYnCryptoCoin (MYN) is the digital asset of the MY™ Brand Ecosystem — a compliance-driven, purpose-built framework quietly supported by B&K Properties NYC, LLC (BKPNYC). BKPNYC serves as the structural component that ensures the coin’s operational and institutional integrity.

As part of the MY™ brand, MYnCryptoCoin is designed to integrate across multiple industries including banking, vault and asset services, naturopathy health & wellness, clothing, blockchain technology, retail, food, and lifestyle ventures. Its function extends beyond currency — acting as a digital signature of trust, quality, and transparency within every sector it touches.

MYN operates on the BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20), featuring a finite total supply of 7,777,777 tokens. It follows an escalating liquidity model with a defined 36-month maturity period, ensuring responsible valuation growth and stability.

Through structured governance, progressive audits, and the legal protection of the MY™ trademark, MYnCryptoCoin maintains ethical alignment with both traditional and digital financial systems. Ownership may be renounced upon full maturity to safeguard community integrity and prevent centralized control.

Mission: To bridge innovation, accountability, and human-centered value into one evolving ecosystem that respects compliance, empowers industries, and honors individuality.

Motto: “It’s about You.”
