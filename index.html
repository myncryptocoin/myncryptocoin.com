What is MYnCryptoCoin?

MYnCryptoCoin (MYN) is the cornerstone digital asset of the MY™ Brand Ecosystem, created to unify multiple industries — banking, vaulting, wellness, fashion, blockchain, and retail — under one compliant and ethically governed financial architecture.

Powered by B&K Properties NYC, LLC (BKPNYC), MYN operates on the BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20) with a finite total supply of 7,777,777 tokens and a 36-month liquidity maturity model. Each phase of escalation enhances transparency and strengthens the base floor of the MYN ecosystem.
